Cost is typically one of the first things person will want to know even before talking design aesthetics. You might hear a few quotes that vary greatly from one designer to the next. How do you know that your not getting ripped off? Thats a great question. Firstly, make sure to set a reasonable expectation for what you expect to get out of your site. For example if you have a budget of $750 and you want $2000 worth of web design work, chances are you will not be happy with someone willing to charge you your entire budget but deliver less than you expect. Its always best to take a few minutes before calling anyone and determine what your goals are with your website.

Believe it or not we have met some folks that just wont even consider the endless benefits of having a strong online presence simply because technology gives them the heebie jeebies! Fear not brave adventurer for our job is to make you feel completely safe and at ease during our initial visit with you. If you're not super duper tech savvy just let us know before our initial visit. We wont list out tons of lines of scary code or any website jargon. Our onboarding process is simple and easy for anyone willing to give us a try! We ask simple questions to get to know your business dynamics. Once we build an idea of how your business works we can translate that into a very individualized website that best suits your needs!

While times do vary from project to project , you can expect a finished design somewhere in the ball park of 3-5 weeks. Of course we do work with expedited timelines as well when the need arises. If you would like to have the absolute quickest turn around time for your project, here are a few things you can do that will help.

Of course we realize that with everyone's busy schedule , creating content is one of those things you may simply not have time to do. Thats why during our initial point of contact we find out as much about your project as possible that way we can have an operational site up and running for you within weeks.
